Our Methodology

Investment Process

A transparent, rigorous approach to identifying and supporting exceptional founders.

1

Deal Sourcing

We source deals through our extensive network of founders, universities, research labs, and incubators. All inbound submissions are reviewed by our team.

2

Initial Screening

Our team conducts an initial review assessing market potential, technology differentiation, team strength, and fit with our investment thesis.

3

Deep Dive

Promising opportunities undergo technical due diligence, customer/reference calls, competitive analysis, and financial modeling.

4

Investment Committee

Final decisions are made by our investment committee, composed of partners with deep sector expertise and successful track records.

5

Post-Investment Support

We actively support our portfolio companies with recruiting, business development, follow-on financing, and strategic guidance.

What We Look For

  • Exceptional founders with deep domain expertise and relentless execution capability

  • Breakthrough technologies that can create or redefine large markets

  • Clear path to defensibility through technology, network effects, or other moats

  • Capital efficiency with potential for rapid scaling

Typical Timeline

Initial Contact → First Meeting 1-2 weeks
Due Diligence Process 2-4 weeks
Term Sheet → Close 1-2 weeks
Total time from first contact to funding:
4-8 weeks
